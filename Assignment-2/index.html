Types of Motor Vehicles in India

Scooter
Scooters are compact two-wheelers with automatic transmission and flat floors for easy riding. They are highly preferred for daily city travel due to comfort and fuel efficiency.
Motorbike
Motorbikes are popular two-wheelers with powerful engines and manual gear systems. They are widely chosen for speed, flexibility, and long-distance commuting.
Compact Hatchback
A hatchback is a small car with a rear door that swings upward, giving access to luggage space. It is budget-friendly and perfect for urban families.
Luxury Sedan
Sedans are elegant four-door cars with separate boot space. They offer a smooth ride, good mileage, and spacious seating comfort.
SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle)
SUVs are large, strong vehicles suitable for highways and rough terrains. They provide high seating, more space, and strong road presence.
MUV (Multi Utility Vehicle)
MUVs are practical family cars designed to carry more passengers and luggage. They are versatile and often used for group or long trips.
Electric Car
Electric cars run on rechargeable batteries instead of petrol or diesel. They are silent, eco-friendly, and gaining popularity for being cost-effective in the long run.
